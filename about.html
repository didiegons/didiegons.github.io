I'm an Information Protection Advisor and aspiring AI leader with a background spanning cybersecurity, forensic accounting, machine learning, and database systems. I thrive at the intersection of technical depth and strategic communication — translating complex concepts into tools and frameworks that are practical, accessible, and impactful.

My work focuses on building AI-powered applications that solve real problems — from protecting small businesses against cyber threats to making machine learning concepts understandable to non-technical audiences. I believe the best technology is the kind that anyone can use with confidence.

Outside of tech, you'll find me serving aces on the tennis court, locked into the strategy of a Formula 1 or MotoGP race, or following the tension of a 50-over cricket match. That same love for precision and competitive strategy drives how I approach every project I build.

What I do

I work closely with developers and business owners to build more secure, intelligent software. On the cybersecurity side, I lead hands-on training sessions and secure code review workshops that help teams spot vulnerabilities early and confidently defend their applications.

On the AI side, I design applied machine learning artifacts, intelligent systems, and portfolio projects that demonstrate how modern AI tools connect across domains like computer vision, NLP, and tabular data.
