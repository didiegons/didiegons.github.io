PromptCraft – AI Prompt Marketplace & Optimization Platform

A curated marketplace to discover, version, analyze, and collaborate on high‑quality prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more – built for individuals, teams, and enterprises.

Quick Facts

Role Product Designer & Builder
Focus Marketplace, Editor, Analytics
Stack React, Node, MongoDB, APIs
Timeline 2025 – ongoing

Objective

Design a platform that helps users discover high‑quality prompts, optimize them per model, and manage their lifecycle with versioning, analytics, and collaboration.

Problem

Prompt quality varies widely, is hard to measure, and even harder to maintain across models and teams. Existing marketplaces lack robust version control, performance visibility, and team workflows.

Solution

PromptCraft introduces prompt versioning, A/B testing & metrics, and shared workspaces so individuals and organizations can ship reliable AI workflows faster.

Value Proposition

Everyday Users

Ready‑to‑use, rated prompts for common tasks across models. Save favorites and remix confidently.

Developers & Teams

Analytics, testing sandboxes, and collaborative reviews to improve quality and reduce drift.

Enterprises

Private libraries, role‑based access, audit trails, and governance to meet compliance needs.

Community

Share, rate, and iterate prompts together; foster learning with templates and best practices.

Process

From discovery to delivery, emphasizing iteration and measurable improvement.

1) Ideation & Research

Mapped competitor gaps: weak versioning, limited analytics, minimal collaboration.

2) Feature Blueprint

Defined MVP around Versioning, Analytics, Workspaces, and Subscriptions.

3) Prototyping

Wireframes for marketplace, prompt editor with preview, and analytics dashboard.

4) User Testing

Feedback loops with students, devs, and analysts to validate usability & discoverability.

5) Iteration

Added scoring rubric, model‑compatibility badges, and guardrail templates.

6) Rollout

Planned tiers: Free, Pro, Enterprise. Private org workspaces with SSO.

Tools & Stack

Frontend

React + TailwindCSS for responsive UI; shadcn/ui & lucide icons for components.

Backend

Node.js + Express APIs for prompts, versions, ratings, and analytics ingestion.

Database

MongoDB for prompt docs, version history, and usage metrics.

AI Integration

OpenAI & Anthropic SDKs for testing runs; adapters for model‑specific parameters.

Analytics

Custom scoring, A/B tests, and dashboards (Recharts) to visualize success rates.

Hosting & Auth

GitHub Pages for artifacts, Vercel for app preview; SSO (OAuth/OIDC) for orgs.

Visuals & Design

Representative mockups of the marketplace, prompt editor, and analytics dashboard.

How It Works

Discover

Search by model, task, and quality score. Use filters for ratings, domain, and cost.

Optimize

Adjust parameters per model, run tests, compare outputs, and save new versions.

Deploy

Pin a version, export SDK snippets, and monitor performance in production.

Impact

↑ Quality
Measured success rate across tasks
↓ Drift
Stable outputs across models
↓ Cost
Fewer retries via tuning & tests
