Smart Travel by AirLytics

An AI-powered chatbot designed to make travel planning smarter, fairer, and stress-free.

Smart Travel by AirLytics is an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist travelers in navigating flight prices, ensuring fair value, and making informed booking decisions.

This project addresses the common frustrations of flight pricing. The chatbot helps users explore travel options, understand fare dynamics, and provides basic support without the complexity of traditional travel tools.

To offer travelers and agents an AI-driven assistant that simplifies decision-making, highlights fair ticket prices, and boosts confidence in travel planning.

I used ChatGPT to design and teach the chatbot. My team sourced real-world airfare data from Kaggle and built a PowerPoint presentation to explain the value. We collaborated across research, UX, and presentation tasks.

ChatGPT, Kaggle datasets, Microsoft PowerPoint, Prezi, and collaborative team tools.

Gives users clarity on travel pricing, reduces overpayment risk, and empowers fair booking choices using AI assistance.

Unlike traditional booking platforms, this chatbot is focused on fairness and guidance ‚Äî not just transactions.

Travelers face price fluctuations daily. Smart Travel brings AI clarity to a confusing space, aligning with ethical AI and user empowerment trends.
