Smart Travel by AirLytics

Overview

Smart Travel by AirLytics is a user-friendly AI chatbot designed to help travelers find fair-priced flight tickets. It assists in planning trips by suggesting flights, comparing prices, and offering booking tips in a natural, conversational style.

Objective

The goal was to create an AI-powered assistant that enhances transparency in ticket pricing and helps users make informed travel decisions.

My Role

I designed and trained the chatbot using ChatGPT. My contributions included prompt engineering, user flow design, and interaction refinement for different travel queries.

Team Contributions

My team members gathered real flight pricing data from Kaggle, analyzed patterns, and created a PowerPoint deck summarizing findings and recommendations.

Target Audience

The project serves budget-conscious travelers, travel agents, and casual consumers looking for personalized, fair, and efficient flight planning support.
