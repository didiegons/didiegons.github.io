Overview

Details

Smart Travel by AirLytics is an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist travelers in navigating flight prices, ensuring fair value, and making informed booking decisions.

This project addresses the common frustrations of flight pricing. The chatbot helps users explore travel options, understand fare dynamics, and provides basic support without the complexity of traditional travel tools.

To offer travelers and agents an AI-driven assistant that simplifies decision-making, highlights fair ticket prices, and boosts confidence in travel planning.

The development of Smart Travel by AirLytics was a collaborative effort combining data science, AI design, and user-centered thinking.

  • I used the GPT-4o Mini-High model to design and instruct the travel assistant chatbot for intelligence, transparency, and empathy.
  • My teammates sourced relevant flight pricing and booking datasets from Kaggle to support price fairness and benchmarking analysis.
  • I engineered the AI’s behavior with a detailed prompt, guiding it to:
    • Consider total value: layovers, refundability, hidden fees, convenience
    • Integrate with APIs, calendar syncs, weather, and price alerts
    • Deliver information calmly, clearly, and supportively
    • Highlight pros/cons of options for confident decisions
  • We created conversation starters to simulate user queries:
    • “Compare these two flights for total value”
    • “Find refundable flights with short layovers”
    • “Can I get deal notifications?”

These elements helped ensure the chatbot was not only functional but truly useful for travelers making informed decisions.

ChatGPT, Kaggle datasets, Microsoft PowerPoint, Prezi, and collaborative team tools.
