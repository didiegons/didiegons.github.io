AI Shield

Artifact 4

Introduction

AI Shield is an AI-powered cybersecurity assistant built specifically for small business owners and non-technical users. As cyber threats grow more complex and targeted, many smaller organizations find themselves vulnerable due to limited IT expertise or budget constraints. AI Shield was created to bridge that gap — delivering clear, actionable protection without the jargon.

Think of it as a friendly security coach that actively monitors your digital environment, explains potential risks in plain English, and guides you through resolving them. Whether it's phishing emails, weak passwords, or outdated software, AI Shield ensures you’re not facing modern threats alone.

Description

AI Shield combines user-friendly design with behind-the-scenes AI analysis to deliver a seamless security experience. It features a clean dashboard with prioritized alerts, recommendations, and links to curated educational content. The tool detects suspicious behavior, risky app permissions, and vulnerabilities that often go unnoticed by the average user.

Users receive real-time notifications when their actions or systems need attention — from reusing passwords to clicking suspicious links. A built-in knowledge base explains concepts like 2FA, malware, or ransomware in plain, accessible language. By learning from user interactions, AI Shield continuously improves its guidance, becoming smarter and more tailored over time.

Objective

To empower small businesses with smart, easy-to-understand cybersecurity tools that reduce their risk exposure without requiring technical expertise.

Process

The design journey began with user research — interviews and surveys with over 25 small business owners from various industries. Many expressed frustration with overwhelming tools, unclear warnings, and a lack of trusted guidance. Based on this, we crafted a low-fidelity prototype focused on clarity and empathy.

After iterative usability testing, we moved to high-fidelity mockups in Figma, prioritizing intuitive icons, readable fonts, and simplified flows. I then built the front-end using HTML and CSS, integrating placeholder AI logic for real-time feedback loops. The chatbot and recommendation engine were tested using GPT-4 prompts to simulate real user scenarios.

This project taught us that security isn’t just technical — it’s emotional. People want to feel safe, not shamed. That insight drove every decision we made.

Tools and Technologies Used
  • HTML & CSS – Built a clean, responsive UI to ensure usability across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
  • GitHub Pages – Hosted the prototype for rapid iteration and user testing with live feedback.
  • Figma – Used for wireframing and high-fidelity design mockups. Focused on accessibility and visual clarity.
  • ChatGPT – Employed for simulated user interactions and generating dynamic AI guidance responses.
  • JavaScript (Planned) – Future integration of behavior monitoring logic and dynamic content loading.
Value Proposition

Cybersecurity tools often cater to tech-savvy users or large enterprises, leaving small business owners underserved. AI Shield provides expert-level protection wrapped in an interface that’s approachable and empowering. It breaks down threats into simple terms, making it easier to take timely and effective action.

Instead of overwhelming users with logs or dashboards, it focuses on what really matters: What’s the risk? Why does it matter? And what should I do about it? With AI Shield, even a solopreneur with no IT staff can confidently protect their business in minutes a week.

Unique Value

What makes AI Shield stand out is not just its technology — but its tone. The platform is intentionally designed to be a security **companion**, not just a tool. Instead of red flags and cryptic messages, users receive friendly nudges, understandable feedback, and proactive tips.

Unlike traditional antivirus or monitoring platforms, AI Shield:

  • Explains problems using real-world analogies.
  • Adjusts language based on user behavior and comfort level.
  • Includes bite-sized tutorials with each alert to promote long-term learning.
It doesn’t just protect — it educates, encourages, and evolves with the user.

Relevance

Cyberattacks on small businesses are rising. With remote work, cloud tools, and limited IT staff, businesses need tools like AI Shield to stay ahead without costly enterprise solutions.
